If Only One Could be My Kitchen

Deducting a few points because of it's menu pricing, I would choose Eddie V's as the "My Kitchen Pick" for Charlotte, NC. With enough variety and unique quality between the surf and turf style menu, good drinks, live music (depending on the time and day) and execellent customer service, this total package choice is a cut above the rest. I will say, choosing a restaurant based only on favorite dishes could result in any of these picks being number one. On this day though, Eddie V's is the victor for me.
