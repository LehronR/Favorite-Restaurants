Restaurant Pricing Dish description
North Italia Prices are standard and not too high. Spicy Rigatoni Vodka or Pizza italian sausage, crispy pancetta, shaved garlic, torn basil, grana padano. Pizza comes how you want or specialty.
Iron Dish Great flat rate pricing per person and all you can eat. Everything that looks interesting. It's all included. Many options to choose from with sides, vegetables and proteins.
New Zealand Cafe Good pricing as long as you don't try to order the entire menu. Special Vegetable Roll Avocado, carrots, asparagus, lettuce, wrapped with thin slice cucumber & light ponzi sauce
Chex Grill and Wings Food is a little pricey but worth it on most days. Cheeseburger or wings One of the best burgers in the city in my opinion.
